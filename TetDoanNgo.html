Capital HaNôi & HoChiMinh City
7/ 6/ 2020 - 14/ 6/ 2020

Lunar New Year

One day after the crop, the farmers celebrated the harvest but the insects that year dragged away the fruit and food they had harvested. People with headache do not know how to solve this bug, suddenly an old man from afar went to call himself DoiTruan.

The tray of offerings to the Vietnamese Lunar New Year

In Vietnam, the Lunar New Year is "Vietnamized" into the New Year to kill insects and worship ancestors. The Vietnamese also call Doan Ngo New Year a "Tet to kill insects" because in the period of changing seasons, changing periods, diseases easily arise. On this day, folk have many customs to prevent and cure diseases.
