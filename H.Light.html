Countdown Lights 2020
Ho Chi Minh City
31/12/2019-01/01/2020

Countdown Lights 2020

Program Countdown New Year's Eve goodbye in 2019, opening the new year 2020 will officially start from 20 pm New Year Eve 31/12/2019 to early morning of January 1, 2020. Countdown Lights 2020 Lighting Festival is staged with unique performance technology moments, promising to bring many interesting surprises for people and audiences to watch the show live on HTV3 DreamsTV. This is the third consecutive year, the biggest arts event in Lights 2020 with Countdown with Vietnamese people at the sacred moment transferring the old year to the new year.

Countdown Lights 2020 happy new year..

With the theme COMMUNITY - FUTURE GENERAL - FAMILY, the Countdown Lights 2020 Festival is expected to be a grand artistic party, meaning to welcome a bright new decade. The public will have the opportunity to enjoy many special performances marking the diverse development of Vietnamese music art, the beauty of community strength, with the participation of artists: Thanh Bui and Son. Tung M-TP, Vu Cat Tuong, Trong Hieu, Ngot, Phan Manh Quynh, Vo Ha Tram, Do Hai Anh, LYRICÍST, BN, MINH & Hoaprox, Tuimi & Datmaniac, with MC Tran Thanh ... Together With official music partner Billboard Vietnam, this year's program director - young director Dao Minh Vu hopes to bring fresh breeze to the people of Ho Chi Minh City.
